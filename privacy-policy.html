Privacy Policy - Sustain Air Technology

Privacy Policy for Sustain Air Technology Private Limited Employees

1. Introduction

At Sustain Air Technology, we prioritize the privacy and confidentiality of our employees' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal data of our employees. It applies to all employees, contractors, and interns working at Sustain Air Technology.

2. Data Collection

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Details: Name, address, phone number, email address.
  • Employment Information: Job title, department, work location, start date, salary, and benefits.
  • Identification Data: Government-issued IDs, Social Security Number, tax information.
  • Health Information: Medical history, disability status, and health insurance information.
  • Performance Data: Performance reviews, disciplinary records, and training history.
  • Attendance Records: Work hours, vacation, sick leave, and other absences.

2.2 Methods of Collection

We collect personal information directly from you, through HR forms, employment contracts, performance evaluations, and attendance records. We may also collect data from third parties, such as background check providers and benefits administrators, with your consent.

3. Use of Personal Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • Employment Management: Managing payroll, benefits, taxes, and other employment-related matters.
  • Compliance: Ensuring compliance with legal obligations, including labor laws and workplace safety regulations.
  • Performance Management: Monitoring and evaluating employee performance, providing training, and managing career development.
  • Workplace Safety: Maintaining a safe and secure work environment, including incident reporting and emergency contact management.
  • Communication: Facilitating communication within the company, including sharing relevant company updates, policies, and procedures.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with:

  • Internal Departments: HR, Finance, and management teams for employment-related purposes.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Payroll processors, benefits administrators, and IT service providers, who assist us in managing employment-related functions.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect the company’s legal rights.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Corporate Devices

5.1 Ownership

All devices issued by Sustain Air Technology, including laptops, mobile phones, and other electronic equipment, are the property of the company. Employees are granted the right to use these devices strictly for work-related purposes.

5.2 Responsibility for Care

Employees are responsible for the proper care and maintenance of any corporate-issued devices. Any damage, loss, or theft of these devices must be reported immediately to the IT department. Employees may be held financially responsible for repairs or replacements resulting from negligence or improper use.

5.3 Monitoring and Privacy

As corporate-owned devices, Sustain Air Technology reserves the right to monitor usage to ensure compliance with company policies and to protect company assets. Employees should have no expectation of privacy when using corporate devices for work-related activities.

6. Data Security

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include secure servers, encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Once the data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information and obtain a copy.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information for specific purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact the HR department.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to employees via email or through the company’s internal communication channels.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact the HR department at [connections@sustainair.tech].

This Privacy Policy is effective as of [23-03-2024]. By working at Sustain Air Technology, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described herein. Additionally, you accept responsibility for the care and proper use of any corporate-issued devices in your possession.
